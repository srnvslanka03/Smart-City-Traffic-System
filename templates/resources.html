FAQs

What hardware is needed at intersections?

IP camera with stable mount and power, PoE or local adapter, network uplink, and an edge device or server to run detection.

Can this work without GPUs?

Yes, with smaller models and reduced FPS. For city-scale deployments or high-accuracy needs, a GPU is recommended.

How are privacy and compliance handled?

We process only vehicle features, no face recognition. Retention policies and masking align with local regulations.
